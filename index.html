Tell us about an early experience with computers or the internet

When I was 8, there was a very popular online game for children called Mole's World. You played as a Mole and lived in this fairy-tale-like world. There were so many things to do: Picking fruits in a forest, taking part-time jobs as a chef, a cop or a firefighter, and buying costumes at a fashion store... It lighted up my childhood.

What will the web look like in 10 years from now?

In 10 years, I believe the design style of web page would be more precise. Designers would take away elements that are not mandatory and only leave the necessary message, but they would definitely make sure the web looks good. I can tell that in 10 years, web could be even more interactive with the users.
